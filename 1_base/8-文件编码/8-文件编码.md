## 文件的读取

> open(name, mode, encoding)
> 
> name: 文件名
> 
> mode: 打开模式，'r'表示读，'w'表示写，'a'表示追加
> 
> encoding: 文件编码，默认是系统默认编码

```python
with open('file.txt', 'r', encoding='utf-8') as f:
    content = f.read()
```

---

## 文件的写入

```python
with open('file.txt', 'w', encoding='utf-8') as f:
    f.write('hello, world!')
```

---

## 文件的追加

```python
with open('file.txt', 'a', encoding='utf-8') as f:
    f.write('hello, world!')
```
